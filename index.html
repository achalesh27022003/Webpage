Message

India is one of the oldest civilization of the world. We have different types of varieties on ground of culture , language , festivals , caste , race , food, dances and heritage. These all

backgrounds and variations tie us in bond of brotherhood , unity , love and friendship. We all are integral part of democractic civilization that promotes peace and harmony.

But in past 2 - 3 decades ,our country is facing many protests , shutdowns and strikes on basis of grounds mentioned above. So, dear everyone it is now high time to preserve

and protect our 'Unity in Diversity' . So ,let's all we pledge to make our country as original version of ' सोने की चिड़िया '.

Important Date

World Heritage Day - विश्व धरोहर दिवस - 18 April .

World Heritage Day is celebrated every year in more than 150 countries all over the world on 18,April established in 1982 by ICOMOS

presenting importance to cultural monuments and sites , aiming to promote awareness about diversity of cultural heritage of humanity , their vulnerability ,

and efforts required for their protection and conservation.
